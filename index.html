Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need a referral to see Dr. Mayur Rai?

No referral is needed. You can directly book a consultation at Rai Ortho Clinic. However, bringing any prior X-rays, MRI scans, or medical reports is helpful for a thorough assessment.

When is surgery actually required for joint pain?

Surgery is recommended only when conservative methods like physiotherapy, medications, and injections have not provided adequate relief. Dr. Rai always explores all non-surgical options first.

How long is recovery after knee replacement?

Most patients walk with support within 24–48 hours after surgery. Full recovery to daily activities typically takes 6–8 weeks, with continued improvement over 3–6 months.

What conditions does an orthopedic surgeon treat?

Orthopedic surgeons treat a broad spectrum including fractures, arthritis, ligament tears, tendon injuries, spine disorders, bone infections, deformities, and sports injuries.
